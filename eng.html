what is "one piece?"

One Piece is an anime about pirates who travel the Grandline Sea. The protagonist is a teenager named Monkey D Luffy, who has a dream of becoming the king of the pirates, for which he went to sea.

The events of the anime take place during the "great era of pirates", which began after the execution of the former king of the pirates - Gol D Roger.

Wealth, fame, power - the pirate king had everything in this world. Before his execution, Roger uttered the words that prompted all the pirates to go to the Grandline. "My treasure? if you want it, take it! I left everything there"
